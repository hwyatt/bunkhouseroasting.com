Our Signature Roasts

Guatemala

Very versatile, and sweet, with layered flavors of brown sugar, caramel, cinnamon-cocoa, raisin, and a pleasantly moderate acidic impression that hints at lemon. City to Full City+. Good for espresso.

Light Roast
Ethiopia

Incredible cup clarity peaks in light roasts, where elegant accents of rooibos tea and airy floral hints are found amidst transparent simple syrup sweetness, along with accents of white honey, and lemon water. City to City+.

Medium Roast
Sumatra

Quite different than wet hulled Sumatra; honey and raw sugar, herbal accents, green apple, ample bittersweetness when roasted dark, sage-infused chocolate, black currant. City to Full City+. Good for espresso.

Dark Roast
Brazil

Bittersweetness holds up to dark roasting, and pairs well with milk. Bodied and balanced, caramelized sweetness, bittering dark cocoa, accented by dried fruit and roasted nuts. City+ to Full City+. Good for espresso.

Medium Roast
Peru

Profuse brown sugar sweetness and moderate acidity for Peru, subtle hints of Thompson raisin, orange, spiced apple and tea-like accents when roasted light. City to Full City.
